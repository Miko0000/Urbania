Backstory
 Rarth grew up as a street kid since his parents were murdered due to a connection with a gang. Because of being forced into this lifestyle, he learned how to provide for himself and deal with the everyday chaos in the city. Surprisingly he was able to survive those years and was later offered a job in the Urbania's Collective, finally being able to become part of normal society... although the Collective is widely hated throughout the public so he tends to keep his identity secret. He still carries a lot of wounds from his past life affecting his personality and overall mental health. 

No longer working for the Collective
