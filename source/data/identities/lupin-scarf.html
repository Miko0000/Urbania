Backstory

Lupin Scarf was born outside of Urbania amongst the poor. His mother died when he was born and his father left him. He struggles to live scavenging the garbage yard, sought to gain most of what he found, thrown out Books, Tools, Electronics.

Electronics, filled with wonders and puzzles mixed by thousands of minds, made Lupin fall in love with it. Led with great interest and guided by the circuits, he spent his life studying Electronics, from simpler things to more advanced form.

He travels from place to place, learning more and more, until he stumbled upon something, a city, the great Urbania, filled with advanced electronics, is a Diamond to Lupin's eye.

With careful planning and almost perfect execution, Lupin manages to enter the city arguably undetected. Ready to learn everything Urbania has to offer.

===

Lupin spent his time in Urbania learning everything, in any way. Occasionally getting into trouble with the authorities for tampering, stealing, or destroying Urbania's property.

He also sells tools, electronics, robots, guides, hacks, as part of trade, though most of it is in no way special, some of them is good enough to attract the Collective's interest.
